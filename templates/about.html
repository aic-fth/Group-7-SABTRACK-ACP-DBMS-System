About SABTRACK

Building a Cleaner, Smarter Community Through Collaborative Waste Management

Our Mission

SABTRACK is a web-based waste management system for Barangay Sabang that empowers residents and officials to collaborate on tracking, reporting, and reducing solid waste in the community.

Our Vision

Born out of the need for a more efficient and transparent approach to managing waste, SABTRACK provides a centralized solution that bridges the gap between the community and local authorities. We envision a Barangay Sabang where waste is managed responsibly, efficiently, and sustainably.

Our Goals

  • Ensure timely waste collection and reduce missed pickups
  • Enable residents to report waste issues such as uncollected trash or illegal dumping
  • Encourage community participation through awareness and education
  • Educate the public via our comprehensive Awareness Hub
  • Provide officials with real-time data to make informed decisions
